VO2 Max

Maximal oxygen consumption of body per unit time

Units: ml/kg/min

VO2=SVxHRx(a−vΔO2)VO_2 = SV x HR x (a-v \Delta O_2)VO2​=SVxHRx(a−vΔO2​)

where

SVSVSV = stroke volume (the volume of blood your heart is moving with each stroke)

HRHRHR = heart rate

a−vΔO2a-v \Delta O_2a−vΔO2​ = arteriovenous oxygen difference (how much oxygen the body is actually utilizing)

SVxHRSV x HRSVxHR = CO (cardiac output)
