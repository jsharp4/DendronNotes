Exercise

Sleep likes:

  • Low body temp
  • Low heart rate
  • Low stimuli

Exercise:

  • Increases heart rate
  • Increases body temp
  • Increases arousal

then lowers all gradually after exercise concludes.

When to exercise to sleep?

Recommended to exercise at least 90 minutes before bed for most people, or at least 4 hours before for people with trouble sleeping.

In general, morning vs night exercise do not affect sleep cycles (though exercise in the morning is a free way to get sunlight to regulate Melatonin)

src: Sleep Foundation (Private)
