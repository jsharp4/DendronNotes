Getting Here

Ferry

Can take ferry over Strait of Gibraltar from Algeciras or Tarifa. In short, Tarifa is a smaller tourist city with smaller trips. Algecira is:

  1. Cheaper
  2. Lower risk of cancellation due to wind.

See FRS ferry from Tarifa to Tangier Ville.

  • Length: 1-hour trip
  • Frequency: Every two hours
  • Price: 43.50 euro

Tangier Ville vs Tangier Med

Tangier Med is 50km east from Tangier (Tangier Ville). Public transit between to is scarce and complex for tourists. To go to Tangier proper without a vehicle, best to take ferry directly to the city.
