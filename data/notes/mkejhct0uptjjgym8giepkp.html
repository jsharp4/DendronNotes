Harding ice field

Big ice field, spans most of the middle of the park - difficult mountaineering (src). Recommended to be an experienced wilderness skier and crevasse rescue.

Also a day hike on the northmost point. 8.2 miles out and back. Near Exit Glacier (accessible from same road). May have snow through July. #day-hike
